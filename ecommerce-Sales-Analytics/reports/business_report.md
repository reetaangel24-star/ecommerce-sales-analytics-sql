# Executive Business Report

## Key Metrics

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value

## Revenue Insights

- Top product categories generate the majority of revenue.
- Revenue trends show seasonal fluctuations.

## Customer Insights

- A small percentage of customers contribute significantly to revenue.
- Repeat customers provide higher lifetime value.

## Product Insights

- Fast-moving products should receive inventory priority.
- Low-performing products may require promotions.

## Recommendations

1. Focus marketing on high-value customers.
2. Increase inventory for top-selling products.
3. Promote underperforming categories.
4. Improve customer retention programs.
